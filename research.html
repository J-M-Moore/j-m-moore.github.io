My work mainly focusses on how functions which parametrise the structure of hadrons (namely parton distribution functions) change if we allow for the possibility of new physics beyond the Standard Model of particle physics. A complete list of my publications and talks in the area is given below.

  • July 2023: Hide and seek: how PDFs can conceal New Physics, with the PBSP team and Michelangelo Mangano. 2307.10370.
  • March 2023: The top quark legacy of the LHC Run II for PDF and SMEFT analyses, with the PBSP team and Juan Rojo. 2303.06159.

  • April 2021: Parton distributions in the SMEFT from high-energy Drell-Yan tails, with the PBSP team, Admir Greljo, and Juan Rojo. 2104.02723.

PhD Thesis

My PhD thesis, Parton Distributions in Beyond the Standard Model Theories, was based on my first four publications, and an unpublished calculation comparing the 'Monte Carlo replica method' for producing interval estimates of parameters with a Bayesian method. It is available here.
